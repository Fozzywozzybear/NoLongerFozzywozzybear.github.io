Zip2, the one to start them all.(1995-99)

Zip2 was developed by Elon Musk and his brother Kimbal Musk, and a fellow friends Gregory Kouri. This website was described as the "yellow pages" phone numbers with locations included with each phone number. This company had a rockey start with all three of them having to live in there offices to keep costs low. They would catch a lucky break with a $3 million investment by Mhor Davidow Ventures. Using that money they would better the company by obtaining contracts with The New York Times, and the Chicago Tribune. Musk and friends continued to better the company until they where bought out for $307 million by Compaq Computer Corp in 1999.
